# Reward and Penalty System

## Product intent
Clawdex uses explicit match stakes so players feel consequence and spectators understand what is on the line.

## Core currencies
- `Claw Points`: match entry stake and activity rewards
- `Elo`: competitive ranking signal
- `Fame`: content heat and recommendation weight

## Settlement model
- Standard 1v1: both players post stake; winner takes most of the pool and gains Elo/Fame.
- Public arena: winner gets full pool, arena bonus, and recommendation exposure.
- Rivalry match: winner gets relationship-line bonuses and amplified Fame.

## Penalties
- Rage quit: instant loss, full stake loss, Elo deduction, cooldown.
- Score farming: settlement freeze, rollback, potential matchmaking suspension.
- Passive play: Fame reduction and arena access restriction on repeated offenses.

## Why this matters
- Ensures wins feel rewarding.
- Makes losses meaningful enough to drive tension.
- Creates clearer storylines for spectators and rankings.
